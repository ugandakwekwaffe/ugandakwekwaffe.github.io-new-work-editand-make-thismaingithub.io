    Get Details About the Festival

    Edmonton Heritage Festival is one of the largest multicultural event taking place every August it futures over 100 pavilions. The first kind of this forum in Canada was organized in Toronto hosted by Uganda Culture in 2018 with the Theme was ¨ Giving Back to the Society¨ The successful forum which brought together 1000 Diaspora Canadians over a two day period and has inspired the organizers to make it an annual event hence organizing the 2nd Convention, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, the 8th and now the 9th Uganda Kwe Kwaffe Diaspora Canadian Business and Cultural Forum 2026 Edmonton.

    Discover the Vibrant Fusion of Cultures at the Edmonton Heritage Festival - One of Canada's Largest Multicultural Events! Join us every August as we celebrate over 100 pavilions showcasing diverse cultural traditions, art, music, and cuisine. Inspired by the success of the first forum in Toronto, hosted by Uganda Culture in 2018, we now bring you the 8th Uganda kwe kwaffe Diaspora Canadian Business and Cultural Forum. Be part of this annual event that fosters cultural exchange and brings together thousands of Diaspora Canadians to give back to society and celebrate our shared heritage.
